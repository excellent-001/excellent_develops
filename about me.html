Jagun Quareeb Alabi

Popularly known as Emini_Excellent

Jagun Quareeb Alabi, popularly known and addressed as Emini_Excellent, was born in Ogun State, Nigeria. He is a student of History and International Studies at the University of Ilorin, Kwara State. Quareeb is dedicated to creating positive change within his local community, Nigeria, Africa, and beyond. His work blends academic insight, grassroots engagement, creative storytelling, and advocacy for the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) with a focus on health equity, youth empowerment, human rights, and sustainable development.

A blog writer, change maker, tech enthusiast, and emerging youth advocate committed to empowering young people and championing their rights, voices, and opportunities. Passionate about leveraging technology for social good, Quareeb integrates digital tools, online platforms, and innovative storytelling to amplify impact. Through his writing, content creation, and advocacy, he raises awareness, inspires action, and drives conversations that put youth inclusion, leadership, and digital empowerment at the center of sustainable development.
