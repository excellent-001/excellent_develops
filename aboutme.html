About Me

Jagun Quareeb Alabi, popularly known as Emini_Excellent, was born in Ogun State, Nigeria. He is a Muslim, Blog Writer, Youth Advocate, Change Maker, SDGs Champion, Tech Enthusiast, and Student of History and International Studies at the University of Ilorin, Kwara State.

Quareeb is dedicated to creating positive change within his community, Nigeria, Africa, and beyond. His work blends academic insight, grassroots engagement, creative storytelling, and advocacy for the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) with a focus on health equity, youth empowerment, human rights, and sustainable development.

He has achieved milestones such as: 1. Pursuing a degree in History and International Studies at the University of Ilorin. 2. Serving as a blog writer and content creator for the Foundation for Neuromuscular Support Nigeria. 3. Advocating for Human Rights through Amnesty International UK. 4. Expanding storytelling skills through Advance by Minority Africa, focusing on solution-based journalism. 5. Being selected for the Accelerate Impact: Express Edition advocacy training program (Nguvu Collective, Kenya). 6. Benefiting from the Yusuf Olaolu Ali Foundation Scholarship 2025. 7. Contributing to the Youth Day of Service (YDOS) West Africa project: Agro-Bridge Africa and Clean Hands, Healthy Lives. and so on...

He still has a long way to go and is open for collaborations. Quareeb believes he is a student for life, learning, evolving, and working towards a future of impact.
